## So many ideas...

As my art skill improves, I find myself taking on too many ideas and trying to make them a reality. I should be working on Tanktop Owl and VERB, but I keep having new ideas of things I want to try out. Because of me wanting to work on so many things, I find myself not working on the other things I originally started with...leaving me in this space of no progress. 

So, I've decided that this summer, I'm only going to work on 1 thing -- a 10 pages based on this game idea I had a while back. It's called Kitchen X Wars. It's a pretty...cliche(?) story about some kitchen utencils engaging in a royal rumble to decide who's going to lead the battle against the HUE-MANS.

I'm primarily using it as a way to practice drawing action scenes and things -- in preparation for Tanktop Owl's next chapter...and as a breather away from Tanktop owl for a bit. 

I'm excited at how my art style is improving -- and how I'm getting better. But, I'm feeling it's time to narrow my focus on trying to build an audience. 

Slowly though...

## Other updates!

No big other updates! School has ended, and summer madness has begun, so I'm taking things day by day. I got an Huion Tablet (finally a tablet with a screen) and I'm seeing a LOT of improvements. I'm very excited for what comes out of this! 

And lastly, once Kitchen X Wars is done, I'll think about where and how to post it...maybe webtoons and weekly releases on TAPAS? It will only be 10 pages. Might also drop it weekly on twitter as a Twitter thread. I don't think it matters.
