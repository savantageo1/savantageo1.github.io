<h3> 3/07/2020 </h3>

Boo me. I have not been maintaining this at all. Nor have I been journaling. I feel pretty bad about it. But, hey. Life hits, and we gotta hit back. I have been working on stuff though.

Working backwards on UT. I am starting to write the lore and history for the story (and I'm enjoying it a lot!) Giving more focus, so I won't be thinking "What do I do next with it". Been making some headway with Jaco's Adventures. I have all the pencils and the page layouts placed. All I need to do now is ink and color in greyscale. Going to release it week by week, in page format.

Swol1s has also taken a backseat...especially since my gym routine is all out of wack. I've come up with something better this morning though, so I should be back in consistently very soon.

I can't wait to start releasing JA. It's been a labor of love, and I finally am getting better at drawing the stories I want to tell.

I also have some other ideas in my brain bubbling, and I can't wait to put pen to paper.
