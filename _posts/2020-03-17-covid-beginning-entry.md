Whoa...day 4 of the quarantine. I don't think I have the virus, but I don't know. Staying my butt in the house though just in case. School has cancelled, and we're moving to remote learning. It's also so wild. BUT, this isn't a diary! Updates for comics and stuff!

Had an idea for a new comic/manga. However, I'm not going to be the one drawing it. I thought it'd still be a good idea, as I've been thinking about Haikyuu!!! and A Silent Voice. I already have a character in mind, and I'm currently working on the script!

In regards to JA, I have about 7 more pages I need to ink. I don't think I have any crazy illustrations/pencils to ink over, so I'm pretty sure from where I am, to the end, will be smooth sailing. I'm pretty proud of how it's all turning out. I can't wait to start coloring & lettering.

Not much else has been done. I signed up for another comics thing, but I'm promising myself it'll be all stick figures. It should be pretty cool I think.

But yeah. We in the house, but we working!
